    Just a Bit About Me

    Hi! My name is Colin. I created my first website www.ccberry.com in my "spare time" as a 4th year optometry student in 1999. I used it to post pictures of my summer in Montana (social media before we had social media) and information about dry eye disease. You can find some pieces of it in the Wayback Machine. And yes, it was ugly. We loved those neon/fluorescent colors and marquees back then.

    The project became too time-consuming, so after a year and a half, I let it go to pursue my professional career. After 20 years of practicing optometry, I have come full circle and come right back to a passion for technology and websites. I can create a vision of your business online. Check out how we have already helped others like you.
